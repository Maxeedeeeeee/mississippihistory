Welcome to Ocean Springs, Mississippi

About Ocean Springs

Ocean Springs is a charming coastal town located on the Gulf of Mexico, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant arts scene, and historic architecture. The town has a rich history, dating back to its founding in the early 1800s, and has been a popular vacation spot for families seeking sun, sand, and relaxation.

Top Attractions

  • Gulf Islands National Seashore: A beautiful stretch of protected coastline perfect for hiking, fishing, and enjoying nature.
  • Ocean Springs Beach: A stunning beach ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and beach volleyball, with nearby facilities.
  • The Walter Anderson Museum of Art: Showcases the work of artist Walter Anderson, highlighting local culture and nature.
  • Fort Maurepas Park: A historic site with picnic areas, walking trails, and a playground, perfect for family outings.

Upcoming Events

Ocean Springs hosts numerous events throughout the year, including:

  • Peter Anderson Festival: A celebration of art and crafts held annually in November, featuring local artists and musicians.
  • Ocean Springs Mardi Gras: A lively parade and festival featuring colorful floats, costumes, and family-friendly activities.
  • Farmers Market: Every Thursday, local vendors offer fresh produce, handmade crafts, and live music.

Local Cuisine

Ocean Springs is home to a variety of delicious dining options, featuring fresh seafood and Southern specialties:

  • The Reef: Known for its fresh seafood and beachy atmosphere, this restaurant is a local favorite./
  • Shaggy's: A casual dining spot with great views of the beach and a menu full of tasty seafood dishes./
  • The Shed BBQ & Blues Joint: Famous for its barbecue and live music, this spot offers a true taste of Southern culture./
